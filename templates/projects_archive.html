RTL-SDR Antennas

In this project I used a RTL-SDR.com V2 software defined radio to receive and decode radio communications from both weather satellites and airplanes.I built or customized three antennas for this purpose.

One was a homemade QFH antenna tuned to 137 mHz made with PVC piping and speaker wire. This was made to receive NOAA APT transmissions. The second was a ground-plane antenna
